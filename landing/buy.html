Buy a device.

Small LoRa radio hardware that connects to the Chicago Offline mesh. No subscription, no account — you own it.

⚠️ Get the 915 MHz (US) version. Devices come in different frequency variants. For Chicago Offline, you need the 915 MHz version. The 868 MHz (EU) variant won't work with our network.
